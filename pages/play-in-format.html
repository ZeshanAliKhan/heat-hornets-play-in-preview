Format notes

What the 9-vs-10 game means

The Heat-Hornets matchup is listed as the East 9-vs-10 Play-In game. That means the stakes are direct: the loser is eliminated, and the winner advances to play again for a playoff spot.

No. 9 seed

Charlotte hosts

The Hornets are listed as the No. 9 seed, so the game is in Charlotte at Spectrum Center.

No. 10 seed

Miami travels

The Heat are listed as the No. 10 seed, so their path begins on the road with no room for a loss.

Winner

Still alive

The winner does not automatically enter the first round from this game. It keeps playing in the Play-In path.

Loser

Season over

The loser is eliminated from playoff contention, which is why the game should carry heavier late-game pressure.
